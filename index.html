wOrK It Out

The IKO Employee Wellness Initiative

The Strategic Opportunity

In the current market, top-performing companies recognise that employee wellbeing is a critical driver of business success. By investing in the health and energy of our team, we can unlock significant gains in productivity, boost employee engagement, and create a direct, positive impact on customer experience. The **wOrK It Out** initiative is a practical, phased plan to establish IKO as a forward-thinking leader that invests in its most valuable asset: its people.

Our Proposal: A Phased Rollout

We recommend a strategic two-phased approach for the **wOrK It Out** initiative, ensuring minimal initial investment and allowing for data-driven decisions.

Phase 1: Active Workstations (Months 1-12)

Introduce a pilot program of active workstations in a common area. This includes equipment like stationary bikes with laptop desks, allowing staff to engage in light physical activity while working. This low-risk first step will visibly demonstrate our commitment to employee health and gather crucial data on usage and impact.

Phase 2: On-site Gym Facility (Months 12-24)

Following a successful pilot, we will leverage the positive momentum to develop a modest on-site gym. The facility will be designed based on employee feedback and will provide essential cardio and strength-training equipment, creating a highly-valued corporate asset under the **wOrK It Out** banner.

The Business Case: A Clear Return on Investment

1. Enhanced Commercial & Customer Success

Engaged, energetic employees deliver superior customer service. Physical activity is proven to boost focus and morale, leading to a more proactive and positive team. This translates directly into higher quality customer interactions and strengthens our commercial standing.

2. Tangible Financial ROI

This initiative represents a sound financial investment. Key returns include lower absenteeism costs, increased productivity per employee, and significantly improved talent retention, which reduces high recruitment and training expenses. A healthier workforce is a more cost-effective workforce.

3. A Modern & Sustainable Reputation

A commitment to employee wellbeing enhances our brand. We can integrate sustainability by using innovative, electricity-generating bikes, creating a powerful story about our corporate responsibility. This helps attract top talent and positions IKO as a modern, responsible employer.

Next Steps

To move the **wOrK It Out** initiative forward, we propose the following immediate actions:

  • 1. Employee Survey: Anonymously gauge interest to confirm demand and inform equipment selection.
  • 2. Costing & Supplier Vetting: Obtain formal quotes for Phase 1 equipment from reputable UK suppliers.
  • 3. Formal Board Presentation: Present a final proposal with concrete data and financial projections to secure approval for the pilot phase.
